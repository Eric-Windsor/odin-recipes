Shepherd's Pie

Description

A Shepherd's Pie consists of a 'filling' of browned lamb mince, peas and diced carrots, topped with crust of mashed potato and baked in an oven.

A good Shepherd's Pie will require a significant amount of preparatory work but the results are worth it for a weekend or special occasion.

Ingredients

  • Lamb Mince (500g)
  • 4 Yellow Potatoes (Medium-Sized)
  • 2 Carrots (Medium-Sized)
  • Frozen Peas (50g)
  • 1 whole egg
  • Milk (200ml)
  • Flour (2 tablespoons)
  • Butter (1 tablespoon)
  • Thyme, Rosemary, Salt, Pepper, Olive oil

Steps

  1. Begin boiling water for potatoes.
  2. Small-dice both carrots, then peel all potatoes and cut the peeled potatoes into quarters.
  3. Boil all potato quarters in a pot with water (until tender).
  4. Put olive oil in a large frypan and begin frying up lamb mince, breaking it down well with a wooden spoon or other utensil.
  5. Add salt and pepper to lamb, and add diced carrots into the pan once light browning has started.
  6. Add thyme and rosemary and continue frying until lamb mince has reached a medium-heavy browning. Stir or toss occasionally to prevent burning or sticking.
  7. While the lamb cooks, drain all potato water and begin mashing potatoes in the pot. Add milk, the egg, butter, salt and a dash of pepper and incorporate them all well.
  8. As lamb and carrot mixture begins to approach final stages, add flour and incorporate well. Ensure no flour is left raw.
  9. Lower the heat slightly, add peas and mix well until peas are just thawed.
  10. Fill oven-safe dish of choice with meat filling, and then top with mashed potato mixture.
  11. Etch a pattern into the top of the potato with a fork. (Optional)
  12. Cook dish in oven for 20-25 minutes until potatoes are golden brown.
  13. Let rest for 5 minutes then cut up and serve. Ensure each plate has a proportional amount of crust and filling.
