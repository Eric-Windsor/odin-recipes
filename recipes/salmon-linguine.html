Salmon Linguine

Description

This salmon linguine is a custom dish prepared by Ashlee as an easier home-made version of her esteemed seafood linguine.

There is a small amount of prep-work required, but it's realistically something you could make on a weeknight.

Ingredients

  • 4 Salmon Fillets (Medium-Sized)
  • Linguine Pasta (250g)
  • Garlic Butter (30g)
  • Frozen Spinach (30g)
  • Salt, Olive oil

Steps

  1. Begin boiling water for pasta.
  2. Chop up all salmon fillets into ideally equal bite-size chunks, keeping the skin on.
  3. Begin cooking linguine pasta in pot with boiling water, adding salt and olive oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Ensure movement of pasta in water to allow for even cooking.
  5. Begin frying up salmon in a frypan with some olive oil and salt well.
  6. Continue frying up salmon until a good crust has formed. Ensure skin is crisp.
  7. Drain pasta water and then add cooked pasta into the salmon pan with garlic butter.
  8. As butter begins to melt add in frozen spinach, mix, and continue to cook until spinach thaws.
  9. Serve up portions into a suitable plate or bowl.
